Vindula Jayawardana

vindulamj [AT] gmail [DOT] com

I'm a Research Scientist at Toyota Research Institute where I focus on the intersection of generative multi-agent systems and human behavior modeling. I obtained my PhD at MIT, working with Cathy Wu. Throughout my career, I have worked on multi-agent reinforcement learning, generative modeling for autonomous driving and sustainable transportation, combinatorial optimization applications, and language modeling.

I have also spent time doing research at NVIDIA and Toyota North America, and software engineering at WSO2, PickMe, and Trancite24. Before graduate school, I was a part of Mobility, Algorithms, and Society Lab at Cornell University and Data Science, Engineering & Analytics Research Hub of University of Moratuwa.
